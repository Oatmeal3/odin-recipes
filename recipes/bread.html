Homemade Bread

Description

Homemade bread has never been easier with less ingredients. The little effort required for this recipe is so worth having fresh, delicious homemade bread.

Ingredients

  • 3 and 1/4 cups bread flour, plus more for your hands
  • 2 teaspons instant yeast
  • 2 teaspoons course salt
  • 1 and 1/2 cups room temperature water

Instructions

  1. Whisk the flour, yeast, and salt together in a large bowl. If needed, use your hands. Shape dough into a ball.
  2. Keeping the dough in the bowl, cover tightly with plastic wrap or foil and set on the counter in room temp. Allow to rise for 2-3 hours.
  3. Refrigerate the dough for 12 hours, or up to 3 days.
  4. Lightly dust a baking sheet with flour. Cut the dough in half. Loosely cover and let it rest for 45 minutes. In the meantime, preheat the oven to 475 degrees.
  5. When ready to bake, score the bread loaves.
  6. Bake the bread for 20-25 minutes or until the crust is golden.
  7. Remove the bread and allow it to cool for 5 minutes. Store leftovers in room temp for 5 days or refrigerated for 10 days.
