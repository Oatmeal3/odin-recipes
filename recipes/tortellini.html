Tortellini

Description

I hate cooking and I love delicious food, so I have my own spin on this recipe that only takes 30 minutes and tastes amazing. It's incredibly easy to cook your ground sausage in a dutch oven along with your onion, then toss all the ingredients in to let it all simmer.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground italian sausage
  • 1/2 of a yellow onion, diced
  • 28 oz diced tomatoes
  • 12 oz frozen chopped spinach
  • 1 brick cream cheese
  • bag of frozen or fresh tortellini
  • 1 tbsp tomato paste
  • 2 tbsp italian seasoning
  • 32 oz chicken broth

Directions

  1. Brown the ground italian sausage & sautee onions in your dutch oven (or pan).
  2. Add all of your ingredients together while stirring.
  3. Simmer for 20 minutes on low heat.
  4. Enjoy!
