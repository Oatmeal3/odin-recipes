Slow Cooker Shredded Chicken Tacos

Description

I am the laziest cook ever, so I am all for a recipe that is about throwing a few ingredients into a crockpot and letting it cook. This is for all my lazy cooks out there that just want some easy, yummy tacos.

Ingredients

  • 3-4 chicken breasts
  • 4 tbsp taco seasoning
  • 1 small onion
  • 28 oz diced tomatoes with green chiles
  • 1 jar of salsa of your choice
  • Optional for serving: Tortillas, shredded lettuce, shredded cheese, guacamole

Instructions

  1. Finely dice onions and place evenly at the bottom of your slow cooker.
  2. Lay your chicken breast on top of the onions evenly spread.
  3. Add tomatoes, salsa, and taco seasoning on top; mix thoroughly.
  4. Allow it to cook for 5 hours on low or 2 1/2 hours on high.
  5. Shred chicken with two forks and mix it back in with your crockpot salsa
  6. Serve and enjoy!
